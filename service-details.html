Bank-grade encrypted deal rooms facilitating institutional asset review.

Secure Acquisition Diligence

When purchasing institutional real estate, verifying structural soundness, tenant covenant strengths, and historical occupancy yields is paramount. Our custom-built Due Diligence Rooms provide investors with immediate, restricted access to verified documentation under strict confidentiality rules.

Each document room is customized to the transaction and includes rent rolls, environmental reports (Phase I/II ESA), building inspections, property boundary limits, title insurances, and active leasing summaries.

Security Features

  • AES-256 Bit Data Encryption in Transit and at Rest.
  • Granular, Role-Based Access Controls (Compliance officers, advisors, buyers).
  • Complete Audit Trail of document views, prints, and downloads.
  • Dynamic Watermarking on all PDF downloads.
